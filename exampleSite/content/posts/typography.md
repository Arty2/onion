The default font is **Alegreya** (serif). Switch to sans by appending
`mod--font-sans` to `params.theme`, or to monospace with `mod--font-mono`.
A `mod--font-local` value disables the bundled webfonts altogether.

Character ranges shipped: Latin, Greek, Cyrillic. All served as WOFF2 only.
